National Championships

12–15 May 2026  ·  Gaborone Stadium

About the Event

The National Championships is the premier athletics event in Botswana that brings the best athletes from around the country together to compete for national titles. Athletes across all age groups and categories compete across track, road, and field disciplines over four days of competition.

Event Information
Start Date 12 Jul 2026
End Date 14 Jul 2026
Venue Gaborone Stadium
Categories U15s, U18s, Senior
Events Road Races, Track Events
