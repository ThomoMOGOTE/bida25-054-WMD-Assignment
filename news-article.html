Team Botswana Shines at Region V Championships

16 May 2026  ·  Championships  ·  By BAA Media

Team Botswana delivered an outstanding performance at the recent Region V Championships held in Lusaka, Zambia. Our athletes secured a total of 38 medals including 24 Gold, 11 Silver, and 3 Bronze — a historic haul that underscores the growth of athletics in Botswana.

The championships, which drew participation from countries across the CANA Region V area, saw Botswana's athletes demonstrate remarkable discipline, speed, and endurance across all disciplines. The gold medals were spread across track, field, and road racing events, with standout performances from both senior and youth categories.

Speaking after the closing ceremony, BAA President expressed pride in the athletes and coaching staff, crediting the result to months of dedicated preparation and the support of sponsors and government. The performance sets a positive tone ahead of the upcoming continental and world-level competitions later in the year.

The association has announced that a formal reception will be held to honor the medal-winning athletes, with details to be announced shortly. Supporters are encouraged to follow BAA's social channels for updates.
